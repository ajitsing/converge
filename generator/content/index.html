CHANGING GEARS: WEB TO MOBILE

Strategies for designing, developing and managing new age mobile solutions

The rapid adoption of smart phones and tablets gives rise to an unparalleled transformation in the way consumers access web and other digital services. This “mobile shift” poses certain questions such as how to create, nurture, and sustain profitable customer relationships for almost every business. Some observers argue that this means “fixed” websites are in for a radical decline, and that in order to be successful, enterprises must adopt a “mobile first” approach and prioritise the user experience on mobile devices. Others maintain that mobile access, while being undeniably important, is “just another channel” among others. To learn and share more about these strategies, we bring to you Converge - Sept,2015 which focusses on strategies around designing, developing and managing new age mobile solutions.

If you have similar interesting experiences which you would like to share, we welcome you to present them in this forum.

Register now to attend the event. As an attendee, you will get to connect with like minded peers and gain insight from industry practitioners.
