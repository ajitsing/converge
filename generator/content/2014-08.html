Business Relevance

The theme for this edition of Converge was “Business Relevance\".

Success for software projects should be measured via the impact that was created in the customer's business. In this half-day event, there were talks and interactive sessions with real life examples that helped inculcate, understand, and measure Business Relevance in team/project. The topics varied from how business relevance can be used to measure ROI in different domains to infusing business relevance in teams' mindset to environment and social relevance.
